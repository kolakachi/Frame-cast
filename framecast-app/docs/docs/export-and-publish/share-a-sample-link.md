# Share a public sample link

Every project can have a public link that plays the finished video (and its scenes) without the viewer logging in — handy for client review or cold outreach.

## 1. Turn on sharing

In the editor's top bar, click **Share publicly** (it becomes **Copy share link** once on).

:::info 📸 Screenshot
`img/howto/share-toggle.png` — the editor top bar with the "Share publicly" / "Copy share link" control.
:::

## 2. Copy and send

Click again to copy the `wyvstudio.com/sample/…` link. Anyone with it can watch — no account needed.

:::tip
Toggle sharing off any time; the link stays the same if you turn it back on.
:::

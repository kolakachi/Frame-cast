How it works

From idea to posted.
Here's what happens.

Step one
Drop in your starting point.

Paste a script. Drop a product page URL. Upload a podcast episode. Or type a rough idea and a hook. WyvStudio accepts all four — so you never rearrange your workflow to use it.

The platform reads whatever you give it, extracts the story, breaks it into timed scenes, and builds a visual brief per scene. Automatically.

Step two
The AI weaves it together.

AI voice narrates every scene. Matching visuals are generated or sourced per scene — AI images, stock footage, or audiogram waveforms. Captions are timed and styled. Music added. The result is a complete, editable video in minutes.

Not a template. Not a slide deck. A real video — built from your brief, nothing else.

Step three
Edit, then post.

Open the editor. Swap a scene. Change the voice. Regenerate one image. Or type an instruction: “Make scene 2 shorter” or “Add a CTA at the end.”

When it's right, schedule straight to YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram — all from the same screen. No file downloads. No manual uploads.

FAQ

Questions you'll have
before you sign up.

Do I need video editing experience?
None. WyvStudio is built for people who want to skip the editing part entirely. If you can type a sentence, you can use WyvStudio. The AI handles everything from script structure to scene timing to caption placement.
What exactly do credits pay for?
Credits are spent only on AI generation — voice narration, AI image creation, and AI video animation. Scripts, scene breakdowns, stock footage, the AI assistant, and video exports are all free. A typical 30-second short costs 250–350 credits depending on your visual mode.
Can I use my own face in videos?
Yes. WyvStudio’s Character system lets you upload one reference photo and maintain visual consistency across every AI-generated scene in every video. No $1,000/year custom avatar. No third-party platform. One photo upload.
Which platforms can I publish to directly?
YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram (Reels and Feed). Connect accounts via OAuth. Schedule posts from inside the editor. A full publishing calendar tracks everything across all your channels.
What happens if I run out of credits mid-month?
You can purchase top-up packs from $8 (500 credits) to $70 (5,000 credits) at any time — no plan change required. Top-up credits never expire while you’re subscribed.
I publish on multiple platforms. Do I re-export for each?
No. From one project, you export 9:16, 1:1, 4:5, and 16:9 simultaneously. The script, voice, and captions carry across every format automatically. Platform format is a toggle, not a re-edit.
What’s the refund policy?
14-day full refund window on all paid plans. After 14 days, any unused video credits are refunded pro-rata on cancellation. You’ll never pay for output you didn’t use.

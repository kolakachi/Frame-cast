The problem

Short-form video is a full-time job.
It shouldn't be.

Writing scripts, sourcing visuals, recording voiceovers, editing captions, keeping a consistent brand face, uploading to three platforms — most teams burn out before they find their rhythm.

⏱
Hours per video
A single 60-second video takes 3–5 hours from idea to upload. At that pace, weekly cadence is impossible.
🎭
No on-screen face
Stock avatars look stiff. Real shoots are slow and expensive. Your videos either lack a presenter or all look the same.
🔧
Tool sprawl
Script in Docs, voice in ElevenLabs, edit in CapCut, captions elsewhere, schedule with a third. One platform should do it.

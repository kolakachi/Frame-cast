Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 27 May 2026  ·  Effective: 27 May 2026

You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do it — either by yourself from within the app, or by emailing us.

1. What data we hold

WyvStudio stores the following data associated with your account:

  • Your email address and (optional) display name
  • Workspaces, projects, and videos you have created
  • Generated scripts, voiceovers, images, and exported video files
  • OAuth tokens and platform identifiers for any social accounts you have connected (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook)
  • Billing records (handled by our payment processor, Paddle)
  • System logs containing IP address and user-agent for security auditing

2. Self-service: delete your account from inside WyvStudio

  1. Sign in at app.wyvstudio.com
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Click Delete Account and confirm

When you delete your account, the following happens within 30 days:

  • Your account record, workspaces, projects, and generated assets are permanently removed
  • OAuth tokens for any connected social accounts are revoked and deleted
  • Backups containing your data are purged on the next scheduled cycle (no longer than 30 days)
  • Billing records may be retained where required by tax law (typically 6 years in the UK), but contain no operational personal data beyond what is legally required

3. Disconnect a single social account (without deleting your WyvStudio account)

If you only want to revoke WyvStudio's access to one of your social platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook) — but keep your WyvStudio account active — do this:

  1. Sign in at app.wyvstudio.com
  2. Go to Settings → Connected Accounts
  3. Click Disconnect next to the platform you want to remove

This immediately revokes the OAuth token and deletes the connected-account record from our database. Alternatively, you can revoke access directly from the platform itself:

  • Facebook / Instagram: Settings → Business Integrations → WyvStudio → Remove
  • YouTube: Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access → WyvStudio → Remove access
  • TikTok: Settings → Privacy → Apps that can access your account → WyvStudio → Remove

4. Email-based deletion request

If you cannot sign in to your account, or prefer to have us handle the deletion manually, email hello@wyvstudio.com from the email address registered with your account with the subject line:

"Data Deletion Request"

We will:

  • Confirm receipt within 5 business days
  • Verify your identity (we may ask you to reply from the registered email or provide other reasonable confirmation)
  • Delete all personal data associated with your account within 30 days of verification
  • Send you a final confirmation when deletion is complete

5. Data we cannot delete

The following data may be retained where legally required:

  • Invoices and tax records (retained for the period required by UK tax law)
  • Anonymised analytics that cannot be tied back to you
  • Any data subject to an active legal hold or regulatory request

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this process or believe your data has not been deleted, please contact us at hello@wyvstudio.com.

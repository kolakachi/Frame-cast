Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026  ·  Effective: 4 May 2026

We built WyvStudio to help you create content — not to collect your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Who We Are

WyvStudio is a service operated by Kollignton Kay LTD, a company registered in the United Kingdom ("we", "us", "our"). The platform is accessible at wyvstudio.com and app.wyvstudio.com. For privacy enquiries, contact hello@wyvstudio.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

When you register, we collect your email address and any name you provide. We use magic-link authentication — we do not store passwords.

Content you create

Scripts, prompts, URLs, and other inputs you provide to generate videos are stored and used solely to produce your requested output. AI-generated videos, images, and voice files are stored in your workspace and deleted when you delete your account.

Connected social accounts

When you connect YouTube or TikTok, we store OAuth access and refresh tokens, your platform display name, username, and profile picture. We use these tokens exclusively to publish content you explicitly schedule. We never read your social media messages, followers, analytics, or any content you have not created through WyvStudio.

Usage data

We record which AI operations are performed (script generation, image generation, voice synthesis, exports) along with estimated costs. This is used for your credit balance, billing, and our internal cost management. We do not sell this data.

Technical data

Standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and request timestamps. These are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • OpenAI — AI script generation, image generation (gpt-image-1), and voice synthesis (TTS-1). Your content inputs are sent to OpenAI to produce AI outputs. OpenAI's privacy policy applies.
  • Pexels — Stock video and image search. Search queries derived from your script are sent to Pexels.
  • Paddle — Payment processing and subscription management. Paddle is the merchant of record and handles payment data directly.
  • Cloudflare — DNS, CDN, and DDoS protection. Cloudflare processes request metadata.
  • Backblaze B2 — Cloud storage for generated video and image files.
  • YouTube / TikTok / Meta APIs — Only used when you explicitly connect an account and schedule a post.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Generated content — Retained in your workspace until you delete it or close your account.
  • Social account tokens — Deleted immediately when you disconnect an account or close your account.
  • Server logs — Retained for 30 days, then automatically purged.
  • Billing records — Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@wyvstudio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

WyvStudio uses only essential session cookies required to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage of sensitive tokens, and role-based access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-app notification at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

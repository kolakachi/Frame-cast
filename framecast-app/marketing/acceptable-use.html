WyvStudio Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026  ·  Effective: 4 June 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes what you can and cannot do with WyvStudio. It applies to every account, workspace, and individual using the Service, regardless of plan tier. It supplements our Terms of Service, our AI Safety Policy, and our Synthetic Media Policy. Where they overlap, the more restrictive rule applies.

1. Who This Applies To

"You" means the natural person creating the account and any other person who accesses the workspace using your credentials. The workspace owner is responsible for the conduct of every collaborator they invite.

2. Permitted Uses

WyvStudio is intended for legitimate commercial and creative work, including but not limited to:

  • Direct-to-consumer brand ads, product launches, and creative testing
  • Educational explainers, course content, and onboarding videos
  • Personal-brand and founder-led content for social platforms
  • Podcast and audio repurposing into short-form video
  • Real-estate listings, local-business promotions, and community updates
  • Internal communications, training content, and team enablement
  • Storytelling, fiction, and narrative shorts where every depicted person is consenting or fictional

3. Prohibited Uses

You may not use WyvStudio, directly or indirectly, to do or facilitate any of the following:

3.1 Illegal activity

  • Violate any applicable law, regulation, or third-party right
  • Promote, sell, or distribute illegal goods, services, or substances
  • Engage in or solicit fraud, money laundering, sanctions evasion, or tax evasion
  • Operate unlicensed financial services or unregulated gambling

3.2 Identity, likeness, and impersonation

  • Create video, audio, or imagery depicting an identifiable real person without their documented consent (see our Synthetic Media Policy)
  • Impersonate any public figure, official, brand, or organisation
  • Use the Service to claim endorsement, employment, or affiliation that does not exist
  • Reuse reference photos found online without the rights to do so

3.3 Harmful or abusive content

  • Generate or distribute child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Produce sexual or sexually suggestive content involving identifiable real people without their written consent
  • Threaten, harass, dox, or intimidate any person or group
  • Incite or glorify violence, terrorism, self-harm, or mass casualty events
  • Promote hatred against people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, age, or national origin

3.4 Misinformation and deception

  • Create content that knowingly misrepresents the words or actions of identifiable people, brands, or institutions
  • Generate political misinformation, election interference content, or material designed to disrupt democratic processes
  • Distribute health misinformation, particularly content that contradicts established public-health guidance on vaccines, medication, or treatment
  • Produce content for phishing, social engineering, scam campaigns, or fraud-as-a-service operations

3.5 Intellectual property

  • Upload, reproduce, or distribute content you do not have the rights to use
  • Bypass digital watermarks or AI-content labels on WyvStudio outputs
  • Resell raw WyvStudio outputs as stock or as part of an AI training dataset
  • Use WyvStudio to train competing generative-AI systems

3.6 Platform integrity

  • Use bots, scripts, or automated tools to create or operate accounts
  • Attempt to bypass usage limits, plan caps, content filters, or our billing system
  • Share your credentials with people outside the named workspace
  • Probe, scan, or test the security of the Service without prior written authorisation
  • Disrupt the Service, overload our infrastructure, or interfere with other customers' use
  • Reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract source code or model weights

4. Connected Social Accounts

When you connect a YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook account to WyvStudio for publishing, you must also comply with each platform's terms of service and community guidelines. Violating those rules through WyvStudio outputs is also a violation of this AUP. We may terminate accounts that repeatedly trigger upstream platform sanctions.

5. Reporting Abuse

If you see content or conduct that violates this AUP, including content shared via a /sample/ link or a connected social platform, please email hello@wyvstudio.com with the URL or workspace involved. We respond within one business day and complete review within five business days.

6. Enforcement

When we determine you have violated this AUP, we may, without notice and without refund:

  • Remove or restrict access to the offending content
  • Suspend specific features (character generation, animation, publishing)
  • Suspend or terminate the account or workspace
  • Refuse future business with the responsible person or entity
  • Report the conduct to law enforcement, NCMEC, or affected platforms
  • Preserve records and respond to lawful requests for information

We attempt to apply the least disruptive enforcement reasonable in the circumstances, but for severe violations — CSAM, credible threats, organised fraud — we will terminate immediately.

7. Indemnification

If your violation of this AUP causes us to incur legal claims, regulatory penalties, or third-party damages, you agree to indemnify and hold harmless Kollignton Kay Technologies Limited and its directors, employees, and contractors. This is in addition to any rights we have under the Terms of Service.

8. Updates

We update this policy as the Service evolves and as new abuse patterns emerge. Material changes are announced via email or in-app notification at least seven days before they take effect.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or how it applies to your use case: hello@wyvstudio.com.

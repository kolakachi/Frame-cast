Why your previous Google submission was rejected

Google's logo policy requires the OAuth icon to uniquely identify your brand. A standalone "W" mark — however stylised — can match too many other apps and gets rejected as "doesn't reflect your identity". Variant B (mark + wordmark in one square) makes the brand unambiguous and is the recommended file to upload.

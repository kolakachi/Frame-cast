Refund Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026  ·  Effective: 24 May 2026

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all subscriptions and credit top-up purchases. If you are not satisfied with WyvStudio for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase.

1. Eligibility

You may request a refund within 14 calendar days of the original purchase date for:

  • Monthly and annual subscription plans (Starter, Creator, Pro, Agency)
  • One-time credit top-up packs
  • Subscription renewals (if requested within 14 days of the renewal charge)

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email hello@wyvstudio.com from the address associated with your account, including your order ID or the email used at checkout. We will process your refund within 5 business days of receiving your request.

3. How Refunds Are Issued

All payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method. The funds typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

4. After a Refund

Once a refund is issued, your subscription is cancelled and access to paid features ends immediately. Any credits granted under the refunded purchase are removed from your workspace.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at hello@wyvstudio.com.

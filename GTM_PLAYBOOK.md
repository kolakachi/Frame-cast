# WyvStudio GTM Playbook

> A disciplined, repeatable go-to-market for WyvStudio. The **cadence is the religion**; the phases tell you what the cadence is aimed at. Context: product is feature-complete, solo operator, distribution is the gap, wedge = **DTC ad creatives** ("branded short-form video without a shoot").

## The one commandment
**One ICP, one wedge, one channel — don't add a second until the first works.** Every "let's also try…" before product-channel fit is how solo founders die by a thousand half-experiments. Wedge is chosen: *DTC brands/agencies that need ad creative weekly without a shoot.*

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (2–3 days, once)
1. **Lock the ICP to a named list, not an adjective.** Not "marketers" — a sheet of **100 reachable DTC brands/agencies** (Shopify stores running Meta ads, small UGC/creative agencies, founders posting about creative bottlenecks). See "Building the ICP list" below.
2. **One message, one proof.** The proof is a **before→after reel** — "script / product URL → finished ad in 2 minutes," made *in WyvStudio*. This single artifact is your ad, DM opener, landing hero, and X post. Make 3–5 vertical variants.
3. **Instrument the funnel** so you can see the only early number that matters: **signup → first video they'd actually post (activation).**

## Phase 1 — Manual traction (weeks 1–4): do things that don't scale
Goal: **10 users who'd be upset if WyvStudio vanished**, not 1,000 signups.
- **Daily (non-negotiable ritual):**
  - **20 personalized outbound touches** from the list. Lead with a before→after reel made for *their* brand/vertical, not a feature list. *"Made this ad for {brand} in 2 min — want the editable version?"*
  - **1 build-in-public post** (X/LinkedIn): a video made in the product + one line on what it replaced (a $500 shoot, a 3-day turnaround). Dogfood — the product makes its own marketing.
  - **Reply to every comment/DM same day.** Speed is your edge over funded competitors.
- **Weekly:**
  - **5 live onboarding calls / screen-shares** with new users — watch where they stall before the "aha"; fix that one thing.
  - **Ship 1 activation improvement.**
  - **Post the week's best user-made video** as social proof.

## Phase 2 — Repeatable channel (weeks 4–12)
One motion will out-pull the rest (cold outbound, a content format, a community, agency partnerships). **Double down on the winner, cut the rest.** Weekly target ramp (e.g., 25 → 50 → 100 activated users); optimize only that channel + the activation funnel.

## Phase 3 — Compounding loops (ongoing)
Acquisition that doesn't compound is a treadmill. Wire these so each user begets the next:
- **Share/watermark loop** — free-tier exports carry a subtle "Made with WyvStudio"; every shared video is an ad.
- **Referral** — surface referral credits at the *activation moment*, not in settings.
- **Showcase** — a public gallery of user-made videos (seed from `/sample/:token`).

## Scoreboard (review weekly, act on it)
- **Activation rate** (signup → first post-worthy video) — the #1 early metric. If <40%, fix the product, don't buy traffic.
- **Week-1 retention** (returned + made a 2nd video).
- **Channel CAC vs LtV** — only once a channel is repeatable.
- **Gate:** don't scale spend until activation ≥ ~40% AND week-1 retention is healthy. Traffic onto a leaky funnel burns money + reputation.

## What NOT to do yet
Paid ads at scale · SEO content farms · a second ICP · partnerships before the wedge is one sentence · building more features. You have a feature-complete product and zero distribution muscle — **spend ~80% of your time on distribution.**

## Building the ICP list — how to actually get 100 named accounts
**Goal:** 100 *reachable* DTC brands/agencies that need ad creative weekly. **Quota: 20/day × 5 days.** 100 hand-picked beats 10k scraped.

### Where to mine (ranked)
1. **Meta Ad Library** (`facebook.com/ads/library`) — the goldmine. Everyone here is *actively running ads* = budget + creative need. Method: search a category/keyword (skincare, supplements, apparel, pet, apps) or start from 5 brands you admire → view their active ads → flag the ones running **lots of video variants** (high creative appetite). Filter to your region.
2. **TikTok Creative Center** (`ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter`) — top ads + trending products; DTC brands winning with UGC.
3. **X / LinkedIn search** — query "UGC", "Meta ads", "creative testing", "Shopify founder", "DTC". Founders/marketers complaining about creative volume or shoot cost are inbound-ready. Capture their handle/name.
4. **UGC / creative agencies** — Google "UGC agency" / "DTC creative agency", Clutch.co, agency directories. Agencies = leverage (one sale → many videos).
5. **Ad-spy / inspiration tools** — Foreplay, AdSpy (free trials) to surface high-velocity advertisers.
6. **Verify Shopify (optional)** — Wappalyzer/BuiltWith extension, or `cdn.shopify.com` in page source.

### Qualify (100 GOOD, not 100 names) — each should hit ≥3
- Running paid social ads **right now** (budget + need).
- Runs/posts **lots of creative variants**, or talks about a UGC/creative bottleneck.
- **Small enough to reach the founder/marketer directly** (not enterprise).
- **Reachable** — founder active on X/LinkedIn, or email findable (Hunter.io, site footer).
- Vertical suits short AI video (DTC physical products, info products, apps).

### The sheet (columns)
`Brand/Agency` · `Type (brand/agency)` · `URL` · `Vertical` · `Decision-maker (name)` · `Reach (X / LinkedIn / email)` · `Signal (why they fit — e.g. "30+ active Meta video ads")` · `Hottest channel` · `Status (todo / contacted / replied / trial / paid)` · `Last touch` · `Notes`

### Tools (keep it cheap)
Meta Ad Library (free) · Hunter.io / Apollo (emails, free tiers) · Google Sheet or Airtable · a web clipper. Don't buy a big data platform yet.

### The trap
Don't perfect the list — get to **100 "good enough" in a week** and start the daily outbound. The list sharpens as you learn who replies.

### Day-1 starting move
1. Open Meta Ad Library, pick 1 vertical you understand best.
2. Find 20 brands running multiple video ads; paste name + URL + a one-line signal into the sheet.
3. For 5 of them, find the founder/marketer on X/LinkedIn and make one before→after reel in WyvStudio.
4. Send those 5 tomorrow morning. Repeat the 20/day until you hit 100.
